# Overview

## Purpose
TicketMaster is designed to supercharge product management workflows. It dramatically reduces the time spent writing user stories, acceptance criteria, and technical scopes by offering a highly responsive "draft-first" generation engine.

## How It Helps
- **Eliminates Blank-Page Syndrome:** Turns a single-sentence slack message or voice note into a comprehensive markdown ticket in seconds.
- **Improves Engineering Velocity:** By generating deep, edge-case-aware specifications, it reduces back-and-forth alignment cycles between engineering and product.
- **Upholds High Quality Standards:** Standardizes ticket formats according to modern product best practices, ensuring UX and tech assumptions are always documented.
